- `FIX`
- `FIXME`
- `BUG`
- `FIXIT`
- `ISSUE`

**Warnings (usually yellow/orange):**
- `HACK`
- `WARN`
- `WARNING`
- `XXX`

**Informational (usually blue/cyan):**
- `TODO`
- `NOTE`
- `INFO`

**Performance related (usually purple/magenta):**
- `PERF`
- `OPTIM`
- `PERFORMANCE`
- `OPTIMIZE`

**Testing related (usually green):**
- `TEST`
- `TESTING`
- `PASSED`
- `FAILED`
